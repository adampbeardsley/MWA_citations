Personal Libraries system in the ADS

The ADS has set up a system that allows to to organize articles in multiple private libraries. This is a useful tool for instance for collecting bibliographies for articles that you write or for keeping track of articles relevant to different study subjects. The system also allows you to attach private notes to individual articles.

The links to the individual private libraries are public links. This means you can give the links to other people so they can look at your libraries and see any optional annotation you may have given to them However, only you can modify your libraries.
